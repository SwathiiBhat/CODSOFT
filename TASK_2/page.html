About Us

At Upahara Kendra, we take pride in offering a variety of South Indian delicacies. From crispy dosas to fluffy idlis, our food represents the richness of South Indian tradition. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a pinch of love, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience.
